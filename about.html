About Us

This website is dedicated to fire prevention in celebration of fire prevention month.


History

House fires specifically cause an average of 2,600 deaths, mainly caused by smoke inhalation. Sadly, 500 children (14 and below) pass away yearly due to the fire, most asleep during it. This site was also created in celebration of Fire Prevention month.


Mission

We are an organization dedicated to helping YOU prevent fires and what to do if you are ever in danger. Unfortunately, fires have become a leading cause of death, regardless accidental or not. We, in turn, volunteer to help hundreds of other citizens avoid dangerous situations.

Vision

We hope to decrease the average of yearly house fire death and save lives. Many families and friends have mourned due to fires, and we want to keep others from the grief.
